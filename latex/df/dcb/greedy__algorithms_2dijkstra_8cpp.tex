{\itshape Quote from Wikipedia.}

{\bfseries{Dijkstra\textquotesingle{}s algorithm}} is an algorithm for finding the shortest paths between nodes in a weighted graph, which may represent, for example, road networks. It was conceived by computer scientist Edsger W. Dijkstra in 1956 and published three years later.
