\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Ground to ground \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Projectile_motion}{\texttt{ projectile motion}} equation implementations. 

Ground to ground projectile motion is when a projectile\textquotesingle{}s trajectory starts at the ground, reaches the apex, then falls back on the ground.
