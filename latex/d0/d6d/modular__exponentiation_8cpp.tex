\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
C++ Program for Modular Exponentiation Iteratively. 

The task is to calculate the value of an integer a raised to an integer exponent b under modulo c. \begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The time complexity of this approach is O(log b).
\end{DoxyNote}
Example\+: (4\texorpdfstring{$^\wedge$}{\string^}3) \% 5 (where \texorpdfstring{$^\wedge$}{\string^} stands for exponentiation and \% for modulo) (4\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}4\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}4) \% 5 (4 \% 5) \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} ( (4\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}4) \% 5 ) 4 \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} (16 \% 5) 4 \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} 1 4 We can also verify the result as 4\texorpdfstring{$^\wedge$}{\string^}3 is 64 and 64 modulo 5 is 4
