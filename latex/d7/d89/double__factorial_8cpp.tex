\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Compute \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Double_factorial}{\texttt{ double factorial}}\+: $n!!$. 

Double factorial of a non-\/negative integer {\ttfamily n}, is defined as the product of all the integers from 1 to n that have the same parity (odd or even) as n. ~\newline
It is also called as semifactorial of a number and is denoted by $n!!$
