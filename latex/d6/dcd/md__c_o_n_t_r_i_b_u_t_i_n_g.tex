Being a contributor at The Algorithms, we request you to follow the points mentioned below\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item You did your own work.
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item No plagiarism is allowed. Any plagiarized work will not be merged.
\end{DoxyItemize}
\item Your work will be distributed under the \href{https://github.com/TheAlgorithms/C-Plus-Plus/blob/master/LICENSE}{\texttt{ MIT License}} once your pull request has been merged.
\item Please follow the repository guidelines and standards mentioned below.
\end{DoxyItemize}

{\bfseries{New implementation}} New implementations are welcome!

You can add new algorithms or data structures that are {\bfseries{not present in the repository}} or that can {\bfseries{improve}} the old implementations ({\bfseries{documentation}}, {\bfseries{improving test cases}}, removing bugs, or in any other reasonable sense)
