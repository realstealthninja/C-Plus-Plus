\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Conversion from \href{https://byjus.com/chemistry/kelvin-to-celsius/}{\texttt{ Kelvin to Celsius}} degrees. 

The algorithm consists on converting a Kelvin degree value to a Celsius value. The formula to convert a Kelvin to a Celsius value is\+: \[ C = K - 273.15 \] where\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item C is the Celsius temperature
\item K is the Kelvin temperature
\end{DoxyItemize}

Check out \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kelvin}{\texttt{ Kelvin}} and \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Celsius}{\texttt{ Celsius}} on Wikipedia for more information about their story, how do they work, when and why they should be used, etc.. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}

\end{DoxyAuthor}
