\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An algorithm to calculate the sum of LCM\+:  $\mathrm{LCM}(1,n) +
\mathrm{LCM}(2,n) + \ldots + \mathrm{LCM}(n,n)$. 

An algorithm to calculate the sum of LCM\+:  $\mathrm{LCM}(1,n) +
\mathrm{LCM}(2,n) + \ldots + \mathrm{LCM}(n,n)$ where $\mathrm{LCM}(i,n)$ denotes the Least Common Multiple of the integers i and n. For n greater than or equal to 1. The value of the sum is calculated by formula\+:  \[ \sum\mathrm{LCM}(i, n) = \frac{1}{2} \left[\left(\sum (d *
\mathrm{ETF}(d)) + 1\right) * n\right] \] where \textbackslash{}mathrm\{ETF\}(i) represents Euler totient function of i. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
\href{https://github.com/chestamittal}{\texttt{ Chesta Mittal}} 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
